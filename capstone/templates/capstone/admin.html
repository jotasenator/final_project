Welcome to ITMT

Welcome to the IT Department Management Tool (ITMT) application.

This is an application that will allow the IT department in a company to manage control of desktop computers and their peripherals, as well as have information about users who are responsible for them.

It will also allow users to inform IT department of any situation that hinders their work through a report.

The above is planned through two types of users. The "admin" user will have all options to manage, inventory and control users, desktop computers and accessories, while users other than "admin" user will only be able to make reports.

At first launch of ITMT application, active user will be "admin" user and through this session necessary user profiles can be created and information given to users: username and password.

Navigation Bar Options

  1. Customize App: When clicked, a form appears where you can edit information brought by ITMT application by default. You can change name, address, email and phone number(s) of company that will be reflected at bottom left. You can change company logo and it will be reflected at bottom right. You can also modify address that it will redirect when clicking on Intranet at top left.
  2. Create User: Here a form appears where you enter data of user who will use one of desktop computers. Required data are their full name, their username, their address, their email, their password, their cell phone number and a photograph.
  3. Users: Where you can see list of users registered in ITMT application. When clicking on a username, an interface appears where you can see a card with information previously added in point 2. Create User form. In this card there will be two options, one to delete it and another to edit it.
  4. Reports: Where all reports sent by users to "admin" are stored in which they manifest problem they have, which user sent it, a brief description of problem and date it was made. These reports will be stored so that those sent last will appear at top. These reports will in turn have a button to delete them.
  5. Create PC: A form appears to enter all data concerning desktop computers and their peripherals, as well as department to which it belongs and who will be responsible for it.
  6. Pcs: Where all desktop computers created in point 5. Create PC are shown organized according to department they belong to and numbered. When clicking on desktop computer name we can display its information and also show a button to edit its information and another button to delete it.
